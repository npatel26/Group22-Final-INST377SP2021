Home Page

Our Mission: To bring jobs and internships to students at the University of Maryland.
This application allows users to browse listed internship and full-time employment opportunities, and allows employers to offer internship or full-time options for the University of Maryland student community.

Our Vision: To create an environment with relevant and easy to access employment opportunities based on location, field, and degree.
